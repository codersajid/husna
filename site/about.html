About Husna Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd.

Established in December 2023, Husna Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd. delivers engineering, construction, and consultancy services with a strong focus on integrity, safety, and quality workmanship.

Our story

Husna Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd. was established in December 2023 as a tribute to the founder’s beloved mother, whose name the company proudly bears. The business is built on respect, care, commitment, and disciplined execution.

We emphasize open communication between all participating parties to achieve successful outcomes—especially on projects where quality, safety, and timelines cannot be compromised.

Quality Management System

At Husna, we share responsibility for continual improvement of our Quality Management Process, ensuring high-quality products and services to our clients.

Quality Policy

Our Quality Management System provides the framework for continual improvement of internal processes, adding value through business processes that support services delivered consistently to predetermined high standards.

Management System

  • Aligning organization through priority setting and action planning
  • Reinforcing capabilities through training and employee support
  • Monitoring progress and closing gaps through indicators
  • Ensuring sustainable progress through internal and external audits

Our Principles

  • Client requirements can always be met
  • Efficiency relies on individual competences and commitment
  • All processes described concisely with trained employees
  • Periodic review for continuous improvement

Our Commitments

The entire line management is accountable for implementing this policy and remains committed to:

Train, lead by example, and guide employees in following processes and procedures
Continuously audit and improve operations
Share good practices and knowledge across teams
Abide by ISO 9001-2025 standards and enhance client focus

Health, Safety, Security & Environment (HSSE)

Our commitment to zero harm, environmental protection, and responsible operations across all projects.

HSSE Statement

All staff abide by HSSE obligations defined in our HSSE Manual. We ensure applicable laws relating to HSSE are respected, complied with, and monitored during entire project performance, meeting Company HSSE Standards and Good Engineering Practice.

Our Commitment

  • Pursue the goal of no harm to people
  • Protect the environment
  • Use material and energy efficiently
  • Respect neighbors and contribute to communities
  • Develop sustainable energy resources and services
  • Promote best practice culture in our industries

HSSE Policy

  • Systematic approach to HSSE management ensuring legal compliance
  • Set targets, measure, appraise, and report performance
  • Require contractors to manage HSSE in line with this policy
  • Engage effectively with neighbors and impacted communities
  • Include HSSE performance in staff appraisal and rewards

Managing HSSE as Critical Business Activity

We manage HSSE matters as any other critical business activity and promote a culture in which all employees share this commitment. This ensures continuous performance improvement and achievement of our zero-harm goals.
