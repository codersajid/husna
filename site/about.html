About Husna Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd.

Established in December 2023, Husna Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd. delivers engineering, construction, and consultancy services with a strong focus on integrity, safety, and quality workmanship.

Our story

Husna Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd. was established in December 2023 as a tribute to the founder’s beloved mother, whose name the company proudly bears. The business is built on respect, care, commitment, and disciplined execution.

We emphasize open communication between all participating parties to achieve successful outcomes—especially on projects where quality, safety, and timelines cannot be compromised.
