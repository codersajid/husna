FAQs

Quick answers for planning, approvals, costing, and delivery.

Do you handle approvals and documentation?

Yes. We support Nagar Nigam/municipal approvals through drawing coordination and documentation support as per project requirements.

Can you provide estimation before starting work?

Yes. We provide BOQ, estimation and costing support to help plan budgets and reduce overruns.

Do you take turnkey/EPC scopes?

Yes. For suitable scopes we can manage planning, procurement, execution, supervision and handover under one delivery structure.

How do you ensure quality on site?

Through disciplined supervision, measurement checks, safety practices, and adherence to approved drawings/specifications.
